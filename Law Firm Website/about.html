Our Story & Legacy

35 years of unwavering commitment to justice, excellence, and the clients we serve.

Our Foundation

Built on Principle, Driven by Purpose

In 1985, James H. Caldwell opened a two-room office on Madison Avenue with a single conviction: that every person, regardless of wealth or status, deserves exceptional legal representation. That conviction has never changed.

Over four decades, Caldwell & Partners has grown into a 45-attorney powerhouse, representing Fortune 500 companies, protecting individual rights, and shaping landmark case law across New York State and beyond.

Our growth has always been deliberate. We hire only the best, we take only the cases we believe in, and we never compromise on the quality of our representation. Each attorney at our firm shares a common DNA: relentless preparation, fearless advocacy, and genuine care for each client's outcome.

Our Core Values

Principles That Define Us

Every decision we make — from the cases we take to the counsel we give — is guided by these foundational values.

Integrity

We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards. Our reputation is built on honesty, transparency, and doing what's right — even when it's difficult.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in every aspect of our practice — from legal research to courtroom advocacy. Good enough is never good enough for our clients.

Client-Centered

You are never a case number to us. We take time to understand your unique situation, goals, and concerns — and keep you informed at every step.

Justice

We believe in the equal application of the law. We fight to ensure every client's rights are protected, regardless of who they're up against.

Innovation

Law evolves — so do we. We continuously invest in legal technology, continuing education, and forward-thinking strategies to stay ahead of the curve.

Accountability

We own our outcomes. If something doesn't go as planned, we take responsibility, adapt our approach, and fight even harder for you.

Our Journey

Four Decades of Milestones

From a small Madison Avenue office to one of New York's most respected law firms.

1985

The Founding

James H. Caldwell opens a two-attorney firm on Madison Avenue with a focus on corporate law. The first year brings 50 clients and a landmark contract dispute victory.

1994

First Major Expansion

The firm expands to 12 attorneys and opens a criminal defense division. Sarah Patterson joins as a senior associate, bringing expertise that will redefine the firm's defense practice.

2003

Landmark IP Victory

Dr. Elena Vasquez joins to lead a newly formed IP division. Within two years, the firm wins a landmark $45M patent infringement case that sets precedent across the federal circuit.

2015

National Recognition

Caldwell & Partners is named to the U.S. News Best Law Firms list for the first time, recognized in 8 practice areas. The firm now employs 35 attorneys and 60 support staff.

2026

The Future

45 attorneys strong, we continue to pioneer in legal technology, pro bono service, and expanding access to justice. Our greatest victories still lie ahead.
